Frequently Asked Questions


How do I create an account?
+
You can create an account by signing up on the app with your email or phone number.


How do I earn Easy Ride Rewards points?
+
You earn points by completing rides and participating in promotions.


How can I add my credit card on the app for payments?
+
Go to the Payments section in the app and select “Add Payment Method.”


How do I become a Captain?
+
Apply through the “Become a Captain” page and complete the registration process.


Where can I get more information, support or make a claim?
+
Visit our Help Center or contact customer support through the app.
